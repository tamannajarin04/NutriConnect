Privacy Policy

How NutriConnect collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective: January 1, 2026 Last Updated: March 1, 2026 Applies to: NutriConnect Platform

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, place an order, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, dietary preferences, BMI records, and health goals.
  • Order Information: Food items ordered, delivery address, phone number, and order history.
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (we do not store full card numbers — payments are processed securely).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, and interaction logs.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and personalize our services:

  • Process and fulfill your food orders and payments.
  • Send order confirmations, receipts, and delivery notifications.
  • Personalize food recommendations based on your dietary preferences.
  • Calculate and track your nutritional goals and BMI records.
  • Communicate important updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions and abuse.
  • Improve our platform based on usage patterns and feedback.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Food Providers: Your name, delivery address, phone number, and order details are shared with the food provider fulfilling your order.
  • Payment Processors: Payment information is shared with our secure payment gateway (SSLCommerz) to process transactions.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of NutriConnect, our users, or the public.
4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • All data is transmitted using 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Payment card details are processed by PCI-DSS compliant payment processors.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Despite our best efforts, no security system is impenetrable. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, please contact us immediately.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on NutriConnect:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions and core functionality.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your theme preference (light/dark/system mode).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling essential cookies may affect core functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.
